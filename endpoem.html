End Poem

Website

A cryptic, open-ended dialogue between two enigmatic entities set within the Minecraft universe, “End Poem” explores complex themes such as the player's role as a dream-like construct, the creators' relationship to their creation, and the cyclical patterns of destruction and renewal. The poem's open-ended nature invites multiple interpretations. Originally written by Julian Gough, linear and unscrollable in the game, this project reimagines the poem with a different visual identity.
